# 📊 Data Analytics Certifications

## 📖 Overview

I successfully completed two Data Analytics certifications that strengthened my understanding of both Artificial Intelligence concepts and Data Analytics practices.

The **Data Analytics Certification from ISDC in collaboration with the Institute of Analytics (IoA), UK**, provided theoretical knowledge of modern Artificial Intelligence technologies. Through this program, I gained an understanding of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Big Data technologies, and their applications in solving real-world business problems. The certification helped me understand the broader AI ecosystem and the role of data analytics in data-driven decision-making.

The **30 Days MasterClass in Data Analytics conducted by NoviTech R&D Private Limited** provided a practical learning roadmap for becoming a Data Analyst. The program offered hands-on exposure to the complete analytics workflow, including data collection, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, data visualization, and insight generation. It also demonstrated how to progress from beginner to advanced concepts through project-based learning and real-world implementation approaches, helping me understand industry-oriented analytics practices.

Together, these certifications enhanced my analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and understanding of data-driven decision-making. They provided both conceptual knowledge and practical guidance, enabling me to build a strong foundation for a career in Data Analytics and related data-focused roles.
